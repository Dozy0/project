2. Transfers of Funds, Transfers to EpicEuro Loans, Bill Pay and Popmoney
You may transfer funds between the EpicEuro checking and savings accounts that have been made accessible to you via the Internet. Transfers of funds will be effective on the banking day they are made. You may also transfer funds from a checking or savings account, made accessible to you via the Internet, to a EpicEuro loan (credit card, mortgage loan, line of credit or installment loan). Payment transfers will reflect on your checking or savings account the next banking day. Posting of the payment transfer onto the loan may take longer.

If you enroll and are accepted, you may transfer funds between EpicEuro and deposit accounts at other financial institutions, brokerage firms, etc. (external accounts). The external accounts must be accessible to you via the Internet and verified and approved by EpicEuro . These external transfers will be processed through the Automated Clearing House (ACH) network and its applicable rules. However, should EpicEuro choose to utilize a different process or the ACH network becomes unavailable, you agree that EpicEuro may perform the external transfer service through other means. Initially and from time to time thereafter, EpicEuro may verify the external accounts by performing a test transfer, in which one or more low value amounts, may be both credited to and/or debited from the external accounts. Once the test transfer(s) are complete, we may ask you to access your external account to tell us the amount of the test credit and/or debit, as well as any other information reported by the external account institution. EpicEuro , in its sole discretion, may require other or additional verification methods. External transfers may be scheduled to occur once, for a future date or on a specified recurring basis. EpicEuro , in its sole discretion, may set limits on external transfers with regards to amounts, number of transfers, timing of transfers, etc. View additional information on limits. These limits are in addition to the limits described below.

If you have a home equity line of credit, you may transfer funds from your line to any of your deposit accounts and you may make payments (transfers) to your line from any of your deposit accounts, subject to the transfer limits described below.

If you have registered for our online bill pay service, you may make payments to third parties from your designated bill pay checking account. These payments are subject to both this Agreement and the separate Bill Pay Terms and Conditions.

If you have registered for our Popmoney service, you can transfer funds to third parties from a designated EpicEuro account. These transfers are subject to both this Agreement and the separate Popmoney agreement.

Because of federal law, you may make no more than six transfers per statement cycle from each of your savings and money market deposit accounts including transfers made via the Internet and any other transfers from these accounts via preauthorized transfer, telephone or check. Failure to adhere to these limitations could result in the conversion of your account to a transaction (checking) account or in the case of savings accounts, the closure of your account. You may make unlimited transfers from these accounts in person or at an ATM.

For purposes of this agreement, a banking day is Monday through Friday, excluding legal holidays on which the bank is closed. If a transfer is made on a non-banking day or after our cut-off times, they will be effective on the next banking day. There must be sufficient funds in your account on the banking day of the transfer in order to complete the transfer. If sufficient funds are not available to complete the transfer, the transfer may not be completed, and you may be charged any applicable fees. If the transfer is completed and there are not sufficient funds, you authorize EpicEuro to debit any of your accounts to the extent necessary to offset any resulting deficiency. You will receive a tracking ID and confirmation message for any transfers you make. If you do not receive a confirmation after requesting a transfer to be made, it may not have been completed. Posting of the payment transfer onto the loan may take longer.
